Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Pehlwan ("the App") is developed by The Zeus Labs. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Account Information: If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect your name and email address to create your account and enable cloud backup.

Fitness & Health Information: Information you provide during setup (age, gender, height, weight, fitness goals, and any health conditions you disclose) is used solely to personalize your workout plan and is stored on your device. If you sign in, this information is also stored securely in our cloud database (Firebase, operated by Google) so you can restore your data on a new device.

Usage Data: We record your workout sessions, streaks, and achievements to power in-app features like progress tracking and leaderboards.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and personalize your workout and diet plans
  • To track your progress, streaks, and achievements
  • To enable cloud backup and restore (only if you sign in)
  • To display an optional public leaderboard (only your chosen username and workout statistics are shown — your name, weight, age, or health information are never displayed publicly)
  • To serve advertisements through Google AdMob

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services, which may collect information as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore) — for sign-in and cloud backup
  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements

Your Choices

  • Signing in is entirely optional. The app is fully functional without an account.
  • You can sign out or permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.
  • You can choose not to join the public leaderboard by not setting a username.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Deleting your account permanently removes your cloud backup and leaderboard entry.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at contact.zeuslabs@gmail.com.
